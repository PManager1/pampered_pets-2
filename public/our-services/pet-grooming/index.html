Pet Grooming

From haircuts to teeth cleaning, Super Pets is your one-stop shop for all your pet grooming needs.

Dog Grooming

Fulfilling your needs as a pet parent is our number one priority. Each grooming experience is individualized and delivered with kindness and exceptional care. We work hard to ensure the safety and quality you deserve and will gladly answer any questions so that you feel confident in giving us the opportunity to groom your loved ones.Â 

Full Groom

The Full Groom includes the Bubble Bath, PLUS the Mini Groom, AND a full body haircut . We will remove tangles and mats, give a soothing bath, and make your dog look like a superstar.

Mini Groom

The Mini Groom is a Bubble Bath PLUS trimming of the hygiene areas, pad hair and feet and face. The Mini Groom is an In-Between groom for when your dog does not require a full body haircut

Bubble Bath

The Bubble Bath will give your dog that groomed look, without the haircut. Choose the Bubble Bath and your dog will get a soothing bath, nail trim, ear cleaning, foot pad trim and brush out.

Additional Services

Spa Package

Includes Fresh Facial Treatment, Paw Treatment, Full Body Oatmeal/Cucumber Spa Shampoo, and Spa Fragrance. All Treatments soak for 10 minutes for full effect.

Nail Filing

In addition to the nail trimming you may want to have your dogs nails filed. This allows us to get the nails shorter and your dogs nails will be more gentle on your skin.

Nail, Pad and Feet Trim

Teeth Brushing
