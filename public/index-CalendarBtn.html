Wellness Center Services

Our experts are here to assist with an extraordinary array of pet services all under one roof.

Grooming Services

Our groomers are highly skilled and will follow your instructions to deliver everything from a simple puppy cut, to specific breed standard groom. Services range from cuts to nail-trimming and filing, hair shedding treatment, teeth cleaning and much more.

Bathing Services

When only a bath is needed, we have you covered. Our products are all natural and soothing to your pets. Our bath and brushout will get them clean and looking freshly groomed.

Spa Services

Pets need a day for R&R too. Treat them to one of our spa packages to nourish their skin and coat to get them feeling their best.

Boarding and Overnight

We want our community to think of us as not only a great place for groom or bath, but also a second home away from home for your furry friend with our boarding and overnight stay services. We have a surprising array of onsite amenities. Call, click or come in to learn more!
